The Deep Thesaurus


Overview

We could say "You are pretty great" or "You are pretty and great" and the word pretty would mean different things. Now, your average thesaurus would recommend the same set of synonyms for pretty regardless of context. This is a bit silly. To make your life incrementally better, we propose a deep thesaurus which uses a deep learning model to rank synonyms based on context. Enjoy!
